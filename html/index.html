About

Hello, my name is Orrin Fredericks. I am the CEO of Fredericks Security Solutions, a business dedicated to providing clients with proper firearm instruction. A little bit about myself, I am originally from Brooklyn, New York. It wasn’t until I moved to Texas, that I began to shoot firearms. After raising my children in Florida, I decided to become a firearm instructor in the Pinellas/Hillsborough county areas. Today’s climate calls for a greater awareness in gun safety. Operating any weapon safely requires instruction and training. I invite you to explore the services I offer and hope that you find instruction/training that fits your needs.

Orrin Fredericks - NRA Certified Instructor (Pistols/Rifles)
